Welcome to Sinhgad Campus

The Sinhgad institute of technology and science, also known as SKNSITS is a private institute in Lonavala and was established in 2011 and is a part of the Sinhgad Technical Education Society. The institute offers are programs in Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering & Information Technology, and ME in Mechanical Design Engineering & Computer Engineering. Admission to the BE program is done through the JEE exam score followed by counseling. The admission to ME courses is offered based on a valid GATE score and the final selection will be done through MHT CET.
